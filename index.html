About Me


Tim Sella was born in Washington, DC in 1993 and was raised in Owings, Maryland. Throughout his youth he regularly participated in creative pursuits and played the clarinet for 10 years. Tim was an avid member of the Tri-M music society, captain of the Improv Team, founder of AV club and member of the National Thespian Society.


After High School Mr. Sella ventured outward and attended the University of Kansas, while initially declared as an Anthropology major- he eventually discovered his love of creating and decided to major in design. During his tenure, his artwork was showcased at several galleries and participated in the University Theater as a lighting, projection and scenic designer. Tim was also a Loren Kennedy Scholar, and was a member of Young Democrats.


As a professional artist, designer, and animator. Tim has experience in theater lighting, animation, scenic design/production, signage and special event features, stagehand duties, event promotion, data entry and customer service. Tim is always seeking collaborative endeavors and is a part of Kansas Cities theatre scene, regularly participates in artistic events like First Fridays.
